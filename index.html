Apex International School

THE SCHOOL WITH INTERNATIONAL STANDARD

Apex International school is a secondary school affiliated to CBSE, Delhi, established in the year 2005. It is situated at Odumbra, in Olavanna Panchayath, Calicut. The school is located in a sprawling 3-acre green campus, 1.5 km from the mini bye-pass. It is a co-educational institution catering to students from K.G to Std X. The mission of the school is to promote education that builds fundamental character traits such as honesty, compassion, courage, persistence, and responsibility. The management envisages a unique school with personalized care to develop intellectual and social skills. The school is an extension of family, bringing out the best in students in partnership with parents. The school values brotherhood, physical well-being, hygiene, and civic responsibility. Co-curricular training focuses on the holistic development of each child, helping them love learning and develop self-esteem.
